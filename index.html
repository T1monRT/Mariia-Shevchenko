About Me

Maria Shevchenko is a Ukrainian artist, based in the USA. Born in Russia in 1982, she grew up in Poltava, Ukraine, where she graduated from the University of Economics and Trade. With a background in entrepreneurship, Maria successfully led her own business in the beauty industry for over a decade, combining creativity with a strong sense of aesthetics. In 2011, she began her artistic journey under the guidance of Anna Flegantova, studying classical painting techniques. Her transition into the arts marked a turning point where personal transformation and reinvention became central to her practice. As a result of tragic events in her country in 2022, she was forced to emigrate to the USA with her son. Since 2024, she has been continuing her artistic education at Orange Coast College (California), focusing on visual storytelling. Maria’s work explores identity, resilience, and the silent power of everyday beauty through a contemporary lens.
